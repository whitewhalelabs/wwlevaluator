Signal Stack

Each module has a status selector, a role definition, and direct links to the live system. Use Healthy, Warning, or Critical to build the live read fast.

Spot Price vs Liquidity
Dexscreener for chart, LP, and pair structure
Open Dexscreener ↗
Use this first for price relative to visible liquidity. Thin liquidity plus fast price movement is a classic trap condition.
Trade Flow and Wallet Activity
Birdeye for trades, flow, token stats, and wallet view
Open Birdeye ↗
Check whether flow is two sided or one directional. One sided flow is often artificial and fragile.
Wallet Concentration
Solscan for holder expansion and concentration
Open Solscan ↗
Use this to verify whether the launch is spreading or being captured. Rapid concentration is future sell pressure in disguise.
Perps and Open Interest
Hyperliquid for perps context and order book activity
Open Hyperliquid ↗ Funding Docs ↗
Rising open interest and aggressive perp activity can turn a spot looking chart into a liquidation machine. Validate price against leverage exposure.
Funding and Solana Perps
Drift for Solana native perp context
Open Drift ↗ Funding Rates Docs ↗
Use Drift when the token is relevant to Solana perp flows or when you need a second perp lens alongside Hyperliquid.
Liquidation Map
CoinGlass and Hyblock for heatmaps and liquidation clusters
Open CoinGlass ↗ Open Hyblock ↗
Liquidation clusters become price magnets. Use them to anticipate where leveraged flows may force the next move.
Wallet Clusters
Bubblemaps for hidden connections and concentration
Open Bubblemaps ↗
This is where a supposedly broad launch often reveals insider networks. If clusters are obvious, shift closer to defensive posture.
Order Flow and Liquidity Distribution
CLOBr for aggregated liquidity across Solana venues
Open CLOBr ↗ Alerts ↗
Use CLOBr to see real support and resistance from aggregated liquidity, not just one pool. This is essential when pool by pool reads are misleading.
MEV and Searcher Layer
Jito tools and docs for Solana MEV context
Open Jito ↗ Searcher Tools ↗
Use Jito context when repeated same size fills, rapid in and out flow, or suspicious bundle behavior suggests searchers are dominating the tape.
